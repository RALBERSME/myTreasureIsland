Courage:



It's about facing fear and discomfort in order to live a fully engaged and authentic life.


Types of courage:


The 5 types of courage are:
physical,
moral,
social,
intellectual
and emotional
courage.

Consequences of courage:



Courage leads to happiness by promoting personal growth, building resilience, and improving relationships through vulnerability and honesty.

Character strengths:


Character strengths related to courage are
bravery,
perseverance,
honesty and
joy of life.

Expression of courage:



Courage can be used as a strength to speak up, take risks, learn, advocate and embrace change.
